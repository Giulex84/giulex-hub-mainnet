Privacy Policy

Giulex Hub is a Mainnet application built on the Pi Network platform. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the app.

Information We Collect

When you authenticate using the Pi Network SDK, the app may receive your Pi user identifier (UID) and username.

Use of Information

This information is used solely to enable authentication, app features, and Pi payment functionality.

Data Storage

Giulex Hub does not sell or share personal data. Any data stored is used only for app functionality.

Payments

Payments made in this app use Pi on the Pi Network Mainnet. Transaction details are processed through Pi Network infrastructure.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on Pi Network services and does not integrate unrelated third-party tracking or advertising services.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the developer via the Pi Network Developer Platform.
